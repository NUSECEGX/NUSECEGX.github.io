Opportunities for Graduate Students

Admission to NUS’s Graduate Program

Application for a PhD program can be submitted online via this link. A candidate is normally admitted at the beginning of each semester of the academic year, i.e. at either August or January. Application materials are available anytime throughout the year. Closing date for the receipt of applications can be found here.

Scholarships

NUS Research Scholarships are currently available. You may also apply for other sources of scholarship to fund your PhD training at NUS, which include but not limited to the following:

  • A*STAR Graduate Scholarship (AGS)

  • Singapore International Graduate Student Award (SINGA)

  • NGS Scholarship (NGSS)

  • President’s Graduate Fellowship (PGF)

  • Commonwealth Scholarship (CS)

If you have the passion to tackle difficult challenges in exciting research areas (nanotechnology, nanoelectronics, semiconductor devices and photonics), consider embarking on a PhD pursuit with us! Prospective students are strongly encouraged to contact Dr. Gong for further discussion on project details.

Opportunities for Post-Doc/Research Fellow

Few post-doc/research fellow positions are currently available in research work related to advanced semiconductor devices and process technologies.

Interested candidates, who are going to complete their PhD or have completed their PhD, shall send their curriculum vitae and publications list to Dr. Gong for consideration.
